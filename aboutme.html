About Me

Sivan Adler
Digital and Studio Artist


The pieces I create vary in media, however they come from the same source of inspiration. I am inspired most by my current mood. I create abstract, classical, and digital imagery based on the things I see and the way I feel. Using colors, textures, shapes, and combinations of all of these techniques, I am able to express my creativity in a unique way. Most of the time, I let my medium take me on a journey of exploration. Not knowing what the end product will look like is an incredible and liberating feeling. Without a precise idea of what I want my art to be, I am free to create without any expectations. This style of creation has lead me to surprise myself in so many ways, helping me discover the artist I am and the kind of style I have developed for myself.

My passion for the arts paired with my web development and user experience knowledge enables me to channel my creative energies into the world of tech and design. I hope to continue being able to use my creative eye to help build innovative, new platforms that are cohesive in design and ultimately solve problems.
